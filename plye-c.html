DIY Install.

plyē's four-direction click-lock is the only patented system that solves the short-edge gap defect — the chronic flaw of every floating floor. One person. One afternoon. No glue, no tools, no professional installer.

Taiwan Utility Model Patent
M677196
No Glue No Nails No Tools No Contractor Reversible
01
Place the first plank along the wall

Leave only a 2–3mm expansion gap — far smaller than the 8–12mm required by HDF floors. Begin your layout.

02
Angle and click the long edge

Tilt the next plank at 20°, slide the tongue into the groove along the long side, then press flat. The long edge locks with a click.

03
Short edge self-locks — no mallet needed

The patented short-edge system engages automatically as you press flat. No tapping block, no mallet. The defect that plagues every other floor — eliminated by design.

✓
Complete a 20m² room in one afternoon. Remove and reinstall with zero damage to the subfloor. Install directly over existing tile — no demo required.
