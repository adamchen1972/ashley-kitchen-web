Specification Logic

The range stays clearer when format and build-up are explained early.

Most families stay on the main architectural platform with two size options. LumiSlate adds a translucent lighting layer, Metal narrows into a single accent format, and DIY breaks out into a smaller adhesive wall format.

Main architectural format
1220×610 mm / 1220×2440 mm
Ordinary, LumiSlate, Cement, Marble, Sandstone, and Rust-led families mostly stay on the shared large-format platform, making them easier to compare across interior and exterior use.
LumiSlate difference
22 patterns × 4 light states
LumiSlate is not just another stone family. It keeps the large-format logic, but adds a translucent build-up and four lighting reads that should be reviewed pattern by pattern.
Metal series
1220×610 mm only
Metal is the outlier: a smaller decorative format for accent-led interiors rather than the broader architectural-format stone families.
DIY adhesive branch
150×600 mm
DIY should be read as its own quick-refresh system. It uses a smaller fixed format and a separate pattern set instead of the numeric SKU logic used by the main stone families.
